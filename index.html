About Us

History · Mission · Vision · Values

Our School

Gyankunja English School is a private, co-educational institution located in Bakulahi, Ghorahi-9, Dang. We offer a strong academic foundation from Nursery to Grade 10, following the Nepal Government curriculum enriched with modern pedagogy.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To empower young minds with knowledge, integrity, and compassion, shaping responsible global citizens rooted in Nepali values.

Mission: To provide quality education that nurtures curiosity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.

Core Values

  • Excellence
  • Integrity
  • Respect
  • Community
  • Curiosity
